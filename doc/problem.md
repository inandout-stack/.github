# Problem

## Overview for Non-Technical Clients

Our open-source solution directly addresses the significant **time wasted shopping**, which is primarily caused by customers struggling to locate products. This fragmented and stressful experience is eliminated by creating a digital map of every aisle and shelf. Shoppers can now **walk straight to the exact location of any item**, from a specific type of almond milk to a broader category of dairy products, without any guesswork.

The platform goes beyond simple navigation by integrating **live product availability** and **marketing insights** to ensure every **trip is both productive** and **cost-effective**. Before leaving home, you can confirm the store's operating hours for any given day and instantly view active percentage-based offers or buy-one-get-one deals that apply specifically to the items you plan to purchase.

While the primary technical focus is on solving the **Travelling Salesman Problem (TSP)** for optimal routing, we offer designated client interfaces and APIs. These are not just for the customer, but for several other key actors involved in the retail ecosystem: Employees, Store Owners, and Brand Owners. Each group is provided with a distinct set of responsibilities, authorizations, and goals, detailed further in their respective profiles.

Keywords: TSP, Customer, Employee, Owner, Store, Brand, Stand, Article, Product

## Key Benefits

- Time efficiency - decrease the time spent shopping
- Cost efficiency
  - Potentially avoid buying unnecessary products (not originally planned)
  - Compare prices within the store for diverse products
  - Compare prices globally of a specific product
  - Live offer updates of a specific store or globally
- Strategic shopping planning - Customers have an overview over different stores within a set range from their location

## Features

- TSP solver
- View and select products from the store article list
- View store offers
- View store availability and operating hour
- Manages the location of products (CRUD operations for shelves, nodes, edges)
- Create and update store details
- Create and update brand details
- Insights over the impact of our application
